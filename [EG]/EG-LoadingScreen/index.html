Quick Start (FAQ)

Q: How to open the phone
A: Press [M] to display the phone

Q: To see all emotes
A: Do [/emotemenu] to display all animations

Q: how to open the inventory
A: Press [TAB] to open the inventory

Q: How to lock your owned car.
A: Press [L] to Lock your car

Q: Default key to talk
A: Press [N] to speak in game

Q: How to use the seat belt
A: Press [B] in a vehicle to put on the seat belt
